Core & Abs Workouts

  1. Physical Health Benefits:
    • Muscle and Bone Health: Regular strength training helps preserve and build muscle mass, counteracting age-related muscle loss (sarcopenia). It also increases bone density, reducing the risk of osteoporosis and fractures.
    • Metabolic Boost: Engaging in resistance exercises boosts metabolism, aiding in weight management and fat loss. It also improves insulin sensitivity, lowering the risk of type 2 diabetes.
    • Chronic Disease Prevention: Strength training reduces the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, certain cancers, and type 2 diabetes.
  2. Mental and Cognitive Benefits:
    • Mood Enhancement: Strength training promotes the release of endorphins, improving mood and reducing symptoms of depression and anxiety.
    • Cognitive Function: Regular resistance exercises have been associated with improved cognitive abilities and a reduced risk of cognitive decline in older adults.
